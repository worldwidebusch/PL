## Purpose, vision, mission

**Purpose.** To make hiring feel like it belongs to the people doing it, not to platforms that profit from volume, chaos, and race-to-the-bottom pricing. ProLinker exists to give great companies access to great professionals, fast, through the oldest and most reliable hiring signal in the world: a trusted recommendation.

**Vision.** To become the world's most trusted referral-based hiring platform where the best companies and the best independent professionals find each other, without the noise.

**Mission.** We connect SMBs with pre-vetted, available professionals through AI-powered matching and a referral-based network so that the right hire happens instantly, not weeks.

## Who we are

ProLinker is the Raya of the professional world. On Upwork, everyone is available and competing. On LinkedIn, it's high volume, low trust, overwhelming choice. On ProLinker, you get in because someone who knows great work thought you were worth recommending — and that changes the dynamic of every single interaction.

**We are not for everyone. And that is exactly the point.**

**Personality (five words):** Independent | Ambitious | Curious | Brave | Reliable

## Who we're for

**Primary audience:** COO or founder at a Dutch or Belgian company, 50–1,000 people. Hires professionals regularly, has been burned by Upwork and LinkedIn. No HR department. High standards, limited time, hates wading through mediocre options. Trusts a good recommendation over a hundred unvetted profiles. Needs to feel: this is fast, these people are genuinely good, and I made the right call.

**Secondary audience:** Senior independent professional (developer, AI engineer, marketer, content creator, or C-level interim). Has outgrown Upwork and the race-to-the-bottom pricing model. Too good and too proud to compete on price against random applicants. Wants to be found by great companies because of who they are, not because they bid lowest. Responds to exclusivity, recognition, and a network that respects their worth.

## Core values

1. **Trust over volume** — We curate relentlessly. A smaller network of great people beats a massive network of random ones every time.
2. **Speed without chaos** — We respect our clients' time above everything else. Fast doesn't mean frantic. It means removing everything that gets in the way of a great match.
3. **Honest confidence** — We say what we mean, back it with proof, and never overclaim. The quality speaks for itself.
4. **Independence as a value** — We believe in the independent way of working. We build for both sides: for the company that needs someone great and the professional who deserves to be found.
5. **Belonging over exclusion** — This is a community worth joining, not a door worth knocking on. We invite great people in, we don't keep mediocre people out.

## Tone of voice — primary audience (employers)

Tone: Confident, not arrogant · Human, not corporate · Direct, not blunt · Exclusive, not elitist · Optimistic, not naive

**We say:** Vetted, Matched, Network, Professionals, Great work, Fast, Trust, Independent, Right

**We never say:** Verified (too corporate), Algorithm (too cold), Platform (too generic), Resources / talent (dehumanising), High-quality deliverables, Quick turnaround, Seamless, Leverage, Solutions

## Tone of voice — secondary audience (professionals)

Tone: Inspiring, not cheesy · Personal, not generic · Freedom-forward, not salesy · Proud, not boastful

**We say:** Free, Independent, Chosen, Referred, Recognised, Remote, Own terms, Life, Work anywhere, Build

**We never say:** Apply now, Sign up for free, Get more clients, Gig, Side hustle, Marketplace, Talent pool, Opportunities, Exposure

## What ProLinker means for professionals

**The guiding idea:** Work from anywhere. Live without limits. Build it on your own terms.

**What we give them:** Projects worth their time, from companies worth working with. A network that grows with their reputation, not their price. No open bids, no competing against strangers. Secure payments and contracts that protect their work. The freedom to work from anywhere, anytime, on their terms.
