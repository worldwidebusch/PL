# ProLinker brandcopy rollout

`prolinker-brand-content.js` is the canonical copy and imagery map for the new brand voice.

## Prepared integrations

- `Prolinker Brief.dc.html`: employer brief copy and AI writing prompt.
- `Prolinker Results.dc.html`: recommended-network language and no fake marketplace-volume claim.
- `Prolinker Login.dc.html`: role-specific employer/freelancer messaging, success copy and Focus Square imagery.
- `Prolinker Voor jou v2.dc.html`: freelancer voice, freelancer photography and work-focused labels.

These mappings are prepared in the catalogue but are not considered live while the concurrent page generator is running.

## Staged assets

- Employer photography: `assets/brand-imagery/employer/`
- Freelancer photography: `assets/brand-imagery/freelancer/`
- Both sets are included in the service-worker precache.

## Concurrent generator note

All canonical `.dc.html` pages are currently being regenerated by another local process. Reapply catalogue copy only after that writer stops; otherwise direct edits are overwritten. The exact copy and imagery choices remain preserved in `prolinker-brand-content.js` and `assets/brand-imagery/`.

## Voice rules

- Employer: confident, human, direct, trusted and recommendation-led.
- Freelancer: independent, personal, freedom-forward and proud.
- Prefer `aanbevolen`, `gematcht`, `netwerk`, `professionals`, `goed werk` and `vertrouwen`.
- Avoid generic marketplace and corporate language such as `talent pool`, `resources`, `seamless`, `leverage` and inflated volume claims.
